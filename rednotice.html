RED NOTICE

Red Notice is a 2021 American action comedy film written and directed by Rawson Marshall Thurber. It stars Dwayne Johnson as an FBI agent who reluctantly teams up with a renowned art thief (Ryan Reynolds) in order to catch an even more notorious criminal (Gal Gadot). The film marks the third collaboration between Thurber and Johnson following Central Intelligence (2016) and Skyscraper (2018).

Originally planned for release by Universal Pictures, the film was acquired by Netflix for distribution.[3] It began a limited theatrical release on November 5, 2021[4] before digitally streaming on the platform on November 12, 2021. Although the film received generally negative reviews from critics, it became the most-watched film in its debut weekend on Netflix. Two sequels, to be shot back-to-back, are currently in development.
